Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is BEO?

BEO (Balance, Ecology, Opportunity) is a blockchain-based token designed to promote ecological sustainability and global collaboration to combat climate change.

What problem does BEO solve?

BEO addresses the lack of transparency, funding, and engagement in environmental initiatives by creating a transparent and incentivized ecosystem.

What technology powers BEO?

BEO is built on the eco-friendly Polygon blockchain, ensuring low energy consumption and minimal transaction costs.

How is BEO distributed?

BEO distribution: 40% for public sales and airdrops, 20% for ecosystem reserve, 20% for project development, 10% for partnerships, and 10% for the team and advisors.

What are the use cases for BEO?

BEO supports eco-friendly projects, incentivizes environmental participation, and ensures transparent donation tracking through blockchain technology.

What is the roadmap for BEO?

Q1 2025: Token creation and deployment. Q2 2025: Public sales and airdrop launch. Q3 2025: Listing on exchanges and eco-organization partnerships. Q4 2025: Full ecosystem launch and global expansion.
